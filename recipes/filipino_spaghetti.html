Filipino Spaghetti

Description

Filipino Spaghetti is a unique and flavorful twist on traditional spaghetti, known for its sweet and savory taste. Unlike the Italian version, Filipino Spaghetti features a rich tomato sauce that's sweetened with banana ketchup and often includes hotdogs and ground meat. This dish is a popular staple at Filipino gatherings and is often enjoyed during celebrations. The combination of flavors creates a comforting and satisfying meal that’s loved by both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (500g) spaghetti noodles
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound (500g) ground pork/beef
  • 1 cup sliced hotdogs or sausages
  • 1 cup banana ketchup
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup water or beef broth
  • 1 cup grated cheese (cheddar or processed cheese)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the Spaghetti: Boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat. Sauté the chopped onions until translucent, then add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Cook the Meat: Add the ground pork or beef to the pan and cook until browned. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Add the Hotdogs: Stir in the sliced hotdogs or sausages, cooking for about 2 minutes until slightly browned.
  5. Prepare the Sauce: Pour in the banana ketchup, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Stir well to combine, then add the sugar and water or beef broth. Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Mix the Noodles and Sauce: Add the cooked spaghetti noodles to the sauce, tossing to coat the noodles evenly with the sauce.
  7. Add Cheese: Sprinkle the grated cheese over the spaghetti and stir until the cheese is melted and well incorporated.
  8. Serve: Transfer the Filipino Spaghetti to a serving dish, optionally garnishing with more grated cheese. Enjoy while warm!
